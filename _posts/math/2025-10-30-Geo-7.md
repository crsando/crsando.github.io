***Solution 2***

硬算就完了，设边长为$y$，$CD = x$，那么$AD = 2x$，所以余弦定理可知

$$
\begin{aligned}
    DB^2 &= y^2 + 4x^2 - 2xy \\
    DE^2 &= (y-2x)^2 + x^2 - x(y-2x) \\
        &= y^2 - 5xy + 7x^2 \\
    \implies \frac{DE^2}{DB^2} &= \frac{7x^2 - 5xy + y^2}{4x^2 - 2xy + y^2} \\
\end{aligned}
$$

令$t = \frac{x}{y}$, $0 < t < \frac{1}{2}$，则上式右边整理为

$$
\begin{aligned}
    f(t) &= \frac{7t^2 - 5t + 1}{4t^2 - 2t + 1} \\
    \frac{4}{7} f(t) &= \frac{28t^2 - 20t + 4}{28t^2 - 14t + 7} \\
    &= 1 - \frac{6t + 3}{28t^2 - 14t + 7} \\
    &= 1 - \frac{3}{7} \cdot \frac{2t+1}{4t^2 - 2t + 1} \\
    &= 1 - \frac{3}{7} \cdot \frac{u}{(u-1)^2- (u-1) + 1} \\
    &= 1 - \frac{3}{7} \cdot \frac{u}{u^2 - 3u + 3} \\
    &= 1 - \frac{3}{7} \cdot \frac{1}{u - 3 + \frac{3}{u}} \\
    &\geqslant 1 - \frac{3}{7} \cdot \frac{1}{2\sqrt{3} - 3} \\
    &\geqslant 1 - \frac{3}{7} \cdot \frac{2\sqrt{3} + 3}{12 - 9} \\
    &\geqslant 1 - \frac{1}{7} \cdot (2\sqrt{3} + 3) \\
    \implies f(t) &\geqslant \frac{7}{4} - \frac{1}{4} \cdot (2\sqrt{3} + 3) \\
    \implies f(t) &\geqslant \frac{4 - 2\sqrt{3}}{4} \\
    \implies f(t) &\geqslant (\frac{\sqrt{3}-1}{2})^2 \\
    \implies \frac{DE}{DB} \geqslant \frac{\sqrt{3}- 1}{2}
\end{aligned}
$$
